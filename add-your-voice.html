ADD YOUR VOICE

This form is for people who want to contribute lived evidence to the Truth Library and Transparency work. Your experience helps reveal patterns that numbers alone cannot show.

Why We Collect Stories

To Ground the Data

Charts and documents can describe what is happening in systems, but they do not show what it feels like to live through those decisions. Your story helps anchor the numbers in real life.

To See Systemic Patterns

When many people describe similar struggles in housing, wages, debt, health, or education, those stories become evidence of broader patterns—not isolated incidents.

To Inform Future Actions

Lived experiences help guide which questions are asked in oversight work and where energy should be focused. They also help us check whether official responses match what people on the ground are actually facing.

Safety, Privacy & Boundaries

Please read this before sharing. We want this space to be as safe and respectful as possible.

Your Choice, Your Control

You decide how much to share. You can keep identifying details vague if that feels safer. Stories may be summarized or anonymized when we look at patterns.

What We Do With Submissions

Submissions are used to understand patterns and, when appropriate, to create anonymized examples for the Truth Library and oversight work. We do not sell your information or use it for advertising.

What This Is Not

This is not a legal service, crisis line, or medical provider. We cannot give legal advice, guarantee outcomes, or respond to emergencies. If you are unsafe, please contact local emergency services or a trusted crisis organization.

Share Your Experience

You can share as little or as much as feels right. Required fields are marked. Optional fields help us understand context, but you may leave them blank.

Important: Submitting this form does not create an attorney–client relationship or guarantee any specific action. It is a way to contribute to collective understanding and transparency efforts.
