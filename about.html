About Global Unity

Who We Are

We are a community of individuals—researchers, workers, parents, and citizens—who believe that transparency is the antidote to corruption. We are not led by any single figurehead, but by a shared commitment to truth.

Who This Is For

  • Workers struggling to keep up with inflation.
  • Students burdened by debt.
  • Parents worried about their children's future.
  • Anyone seeking to understand the systems that govern them.

What This Is Not

  • Not a political party.
  • Not a cult.
  • Not telling anyone how to vote.
  • Not financial or legal advice.

How We Protect You (Safety & Privacy)

  • No Data Selling: We do not sell your data to anyone.
  • Minimal Tracking: We only use basic analytics for site performance.
  • Right to Vanish: If you submit a story, you can request its removal at any time.

Methods & Sources

We rely on public data found in government reports (Fed, BLS, CBO), public economic series, and historical records. We do not traffic in illegal leaks or private surveillance. Our power comes from organizing public information that is usually hidden in plain sight.
