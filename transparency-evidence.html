TRANSPARENCY & EVIDENCE

This page is the front door into the documents, charts, transcripts, and data behind the Truth Library. Every tile points to a folder in /docs where evidence can live, so anyone—citizen, journalist, or official—can verify claims for themselves.

How to Use This Page

1 · Pick a Topic

Choose the area you care about most: monetary policy, housing, Congress behavior, agencies, or human impact. Each category groups related documents together so you don’t have to hunt.

2 · Open the Evidence Folder

Each tile links into a dedicated folder under /docs with reports, charts, and notes. These start as placeholders and grow over time as more material is added.

3 · Cross-Check With the Truth Library

Use the Truth Library rooms to understand the patterns, then use this page to see the receipts. Together, they form a Mirror: understanding plus verification.
