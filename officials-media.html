For Officials & Media

We invite lawful, respectful engagement from public servants, journalists, and researchers. This page provides a concise overview of what Global Unity is, how to use our materials, and where to find key evidence.

For Public Officials

This platform exists to aggregate public data and sentiment. We invite you to review the Truth Library to understand the systemic concerns of your constituents. We are committed to lawful petitioning, transparency, and good-faith dialogue.

For Journalists & Researchers

You are free to use the data maps and document collections found in Transparency & Evidence. We ask that you credit “Global Unity” when citing our specific aggregations or narrative frameworks.

Key Links for Review

These links provide a fast way to understand what Global Unity is doing and how the work is organized.

  • The Truth Library framework — our explanation of systemic patterns.
  • Transparency & Evidence — entry points into public data and documents.
  • Source documents (/docs) — raw PDFs, notes, and datasets used in analysis.
  • Our methodology & sources — how we select and interpret evidence.
  • Our Actions — high-level map of oversight and APA-related work.

Use, Attribution & Limits

  • Use: You may quote, screenshot, or link to this site and its public documents as part of your reporting or official review.
  • Attribution: When using our organized charts, tables, or narrative frameworks, please reference “Global Unity (global-unity.netlify.app)” as the source.
  • Independence: We are not affiliated with any political party, government agency, or corporate interest. We do not accept paid advertising.
  • Limits: Our summaries are built on public data. They should not be treated as personalized legal, financial, or medical advice.
